Whomwah Wordpress Theme

A Wordpress theme as seen on whomwah.com

Whomwah Wordpress Theme is as you may expect a Wordpress theme that you can add to your Wordpress enabled site. It worked on my old 2.6 version and works fine on my new 2.7 install.
