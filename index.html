Whomwah Wordpress Theme

A Wordpress theme as seen on whomwah.com

Whomwah Wordpress Theme is as you may expect a Wordpress theme that you can add to you Wordpress enabled site. It worked on my old 2.6 version and works fine on my new 2.7 install.

  • Download Whomwah 1.0 (tar) (3.9 KB)
